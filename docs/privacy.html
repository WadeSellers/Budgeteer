Budgeteer Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

The short version: Your financial data stays on your device. When you use voice recording, only the text transcript (not audio) is briefly sent to an AI service to extract the purchase amount and category. Nothing is stored on our servers.

What Budgeteer Does

Budgeteer is a budget tracking app that lets you log purchases by voice. You hold a button, say what you bought, and the app figures out the amount, description, and category automatically.

Data Stored on Your Device

  • Transactions — amount, description, category, and timestamp for each purchase you log
  • Budget settings — your monthly budget amount and app preferences (theme, etc.)

This data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It is encrypted at rest by iOS.

Data Sent to Third Parties

When you record a purchase by voice, the following happens:

  1. Apple's on-device speech recognition converts your voice to text
  2. The text transcript (e.g., "twelve fifty at Trader Joe's") is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to extract the dollar amount, description, and spending category
  3. The structured result is returned and saved to your device

What is sent: Only the text transcript of what you said. No audio, no personal identifiers, no device information, no financial account details.

What is NOT sent: Your name, email, location, contacts, photos, browsing history, or any data beyond the purchase description you spoke.

Anthropic processes this data under their privacy policy. API requests are not used to train their models.

Audio Recording

Budgeteer accesses your microphone only while you hold the record button. Audio is processed in real-time by Apple's on-device speech recognizer and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. Only the resulting text transcript is used.

Analytics & Tracking

Budgeteer does not include any analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, advertising identifiers, or telemetry. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or behavioral metrics.

Data Retention

  • On your device: Transaction data is kept until you delete it (individually via the edit screen, or by deleting the app)
  • Anthropic API: Text transcripts are processed transiently and are not retained by Anthropic for API customers

Your Rights

  • Delete individual transactions at any time within the app
  • Delete all data by uninstalling Budgeteer
  • Deny microphone access — you can still log purchases manually via the keyboard

Children's Privacy

Budgeteer is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we'll update this page and the "last updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
